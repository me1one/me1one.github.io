Usage

PeepoHappy is a Twitch Emote which is meant to show happiness or joy (also see widepeepoHappy).

History

PeepoHappy is an Emote based on Pepe the Frog, a cartoon by Matt Furie. It was first uploaded on BetterTTV on November 23rd 2017 by Seremendal, then to FrankerFaceZ on December 22nd 2017 by Klotzi and finally to 7TV on May 28th 2021 by brian6932.
